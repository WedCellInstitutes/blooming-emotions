About Us


Blooming Emotions is a premier event management company offering a comprehensive range of services for successful events in Delhi NCR and beyond. Our 1-acre warehouse houses everything from fabrics and furniture to flowers and set structures, giving us total control over quality and client satisfaction. With the capacity to handle multiple events in a single day, we pride ourselves on our uncompromising attitude and exceptional service.


With years of experience, our dedicated team meticulously plans and executes each detail, ensuring seamless and unforgettable events. Whether it’s a grand wedding, corporate event, or intimate gathering, we bring your vision to life with creativity, innovation, and attention to detail. Trust Blooming Emotions to transform your special occasions into extraordinary celebrations, delivering unmatched expertise and unwavering dedication to quality.

Why Choose Us


We believe that the decor at an event is the most integral part for setting the mood, framing the emotion and underscoring the importance of the event.


  • We will take care of everything – flowers, candles, drapes, structures, stage, while you sit back and relax.
  • We will select from a wide range of themes or customize concepts that best suits the mood of the event.
  • We make sure your event is seamless and stress free
  • We are with you throughout the way from planning to execution
  • We will keep track of everything from vendor contacts to the to-dos
  • Are you looking for something formal and elegant or modern and young? Either way, Bling Blush is the pick for you
  • We custom design every detail of your event, to make sure it is as unique as you are
  • Be assured, your special day will be remembered by all. We are your one stop shop for all things events
